## Project_Overview
This project analyzes New Year sales data to identify patterns, customer behavior, and revenue trends. 

The analysis includes:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Visualizations and insights on gender, age group, states, marital status, occupation, and product categories

This project helps businesses understand their customers better, target high-purchasing segments, and optimize sales and marketing strategies during seasonal events.

## üîç Exploratory_Data_Analysis (EDA)
EDA is performed to understand patterns and trends in the New Year sales dataset. 
It helps identify high-purchasing customer groups and popular product categories.

**Analysis Focus:**
- Gender: Compare purchase frequency and total amount
- Age Group: Identify the most active age segments
- States: Analyze top-performing states by order count and revenue
- Marital Status: Study spending patterns
- Occupation: Identify occupations contributing most to sales
- Product Categories: Most ordered and highest revenue-generating products

**Key Insights:**
- Female customers purchase more than males.
- Most active age group: 26‚Äì35 years.
- Highest orders: UP (1800+), Maharashtra (1500+); highest revenue: UP (~1.75M), Maharashtra (~1.4M).
- Unmarried customers (0) spend more; females lead in spending.
- IT sector contributes most to orders and revenue.
- Most orders: Clothing & Apparel; highest revenue: Food items.
